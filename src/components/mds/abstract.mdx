# TL;DR
We introduce Persona-E², a human-grounded dataset for personality-shaped emotion analysis, and demonstrate that trait-aware modeling—particularly using the Big Five—helps LLMs mitigate ``personality illusion'' in simulating reader appraisals.
# Abstract
Persona-E² is a large-scale dataset designed to explore the complex interplay between persona, events, and emotional responses. Understanding how individual personalities influence emotional reactions to various life events, news, and social media content is crucial for developing more empathetic and human-like AI systems. This dataset provides expert-annotated events coupled with detailed persona profiles and their corresponding emotional states, enabling research into persona-driven emotion prediction and empathetic response generation. Our analysis reveals that persona information significantly enhances the accuracy of emotion prediction models across diverse scenarios.
